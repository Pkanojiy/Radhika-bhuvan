About Us

Our Story

Since 2001, Radhika Bhavan has been the heart of celebrations on Waghodia Road, Vadodara.

For 25 proud years, we have hosted over 1000+ happy weddings and countless joyful occasions, earning a 4.6-star rating on Google — the highest on Waghodia Road and among the Top 10 banquet halls in Vadodara.

As one of the oldest and most trusted names in the area, we believe every celebration deserves perfection. From grand weddings to intimate family functions, our elegant halls, warm hospitality, and attention to every little detail make each event truly unforgettable.

At Radhika Bhavan, you don't just book a venue — you become part of our family. Our mission is simple: to make your special day as perfect as you've always imagined.

A Word From The Owner

Built With Love, Since 2001

Dear friends,

In 2001, I opened the doors of a small ground-floor hall with nothing but a dream — that every family in Waghodia should have a graceful place to celebrate their happiest days. I still remember our very first wedding: the lights, the music, the tears of joy.

Twenty-five years and more than a thousand weddings later, that dream has grown into three banquet halls and fifteen guest rooms — but our heart remains exactly the same. When you celebrate with us, you are not a customer. You are family.

With gratitude,
